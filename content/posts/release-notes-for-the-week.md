Not every review needs to be formal. Sometimes the simplest version is the most helpful: what changed, what felt good, and what should happen again.

## A compact format

- Wins: list the things that worked.
- Friction: name the parts that felt heavy.
- Next: choose one or two adjustments.

This is enough to create a paper trail for growth without crushing the week under its own structure.

### One small snippet

```bash
printf '%s\n' "keep going" "stay kind" "repeat the good parts"
```

A blog archive works in a similar way. Over time, it becomes a record of attention.
